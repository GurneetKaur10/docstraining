Presentation day:
There were total 7 teams. All worked good and displayed their projects and explained them very well.
We also presented our project and explained it.
After all the presentations, Jaswant sir taught some key points for giving a presentation.
KEY POINTS WERE AS FOLLOW:
1. Always keep a diary with you while giving a presentation.
2. Introduce yourself professionally (Name, designation- DevOps Engineer)
3. Start by telling the tasks to be done to make the project.
4. Then tell the errors you faced and how you resolved them.
5. At last, end your presentation by asking audience if they have any queries?

At last, voting was conducted in teams to decide the best project among all.
It was truly a wonderful and motivating moment for our team to have our project selected as the best one.
